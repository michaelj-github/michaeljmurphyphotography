About Me

Hello, I’m Michael J Murphy, a passionate nature and bird photographer based in Cincinnati, Ohio. My mission is to capture moments of tranquility and natural beauty to share with others and inspire appreciation for the outdoors.

My camera is an OM SYSTEM OM-1 Mark II with a 12-40mm f/2.8 Lens. I'm just learning how to use it and I'm eager to get a telephoto zoom lens for longer shots of birds. I'm looking at the M.Zuiko 100-400mm F5-6.3 IS as it looks like a great lens and a moderate price.
